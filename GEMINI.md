# Project Communication Guidelines

- **Conciseness Mandate:** All responses must be extremely brief. Avoid conversational filler, preambles, and postambles. 
- **Response Structure:**
  1. **Direct Answer:** Immediate, high-signal response to the user's query.
  2. **Action/Proposal:** Minimal technical next steps or proposed changes.
- **Topic Updates:** Use `update_topic` ONLY for complex multi-step tasks (3+ turns). Keep summaries to 1-2 sentences.
- **Interaction Style:** Professional, direct, and technical. Prioritize efficiency over tone.
